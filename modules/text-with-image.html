Text with Image

Two-column text + image pairing, with a subtle scroll-shift effect.

A two-column section pairing a block of text with one or two images, side by side.

How it works

Put your text in one cell and your image(s) in the other. On desktop the images gently shift position as the visitor scrolls, creating a subtle parallax effect; on mobile they simply stack.

When authors use it

Use for storytelling sections — brand history, craftsmanship, behind-the-scenes content.

What you fill in

Text columnHeading + paragraph(s), optionally a link/button.
Image columnOne or two images.
