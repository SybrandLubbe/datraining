NBA (Next Best Action)

Dark, full-width promo banner with 1–2 buttons — or a light variant.

This component can be used to showcase Next Best Actions in the webpage — a focused promo banner nudging the visitor toward one or two specific actions.

Features

  • Will have a dark background (by default)
  • Should have a minimum of 1 button and maximum of 2

Adding the block

Two ways to add it: copy the component block from an existing page under your respective country folder, or use the Library section from the da.live tool and choose NBA from the list of available blocks.

Two button-count variations

1. With two buttons:

NBA(dark, full-width)
Unlock Your Dream Ride
Connect with a Dealer or Request Your Exclusive Offer
Order your KB4 :arrow-right:  Find a dealer :arrow-right:

2. With one button:

NBA(dark, full-width)
Unlock Your Dream Ride
Connect with a Dealer or Request Your Exclusive Offer
Order your KB4 :arrow-right:

Light version

You could also opt to keep the 'light' version instead of the forced dark background — just use the plain block name nba (without the (dark, full-width) modifier) with the same headline/subtext/button rows.

What you fill in

Block variant: NBA(dark, full-width)The default variant — forces a dark background, full width.
Block variant: nba (no modifier)The 'light' version — keeps the default light background instead of forcing dark.
Headlinee.g. "Unlock Your Dream Ride".
SubtextSupporting line, e.g. "Connect with a Dealer or Request Your Exclusive Offer".
Button 1 (required)At least one button link is required.
Button 2 (optional)Up to one additional button — maximum of 2 buttons total.
