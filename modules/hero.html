Hero

Full-width banner: background image/video, heading, subheading, optional CTA.

A full-width banner, almost always the first thing on a page: a background image or video, a heading, optional subheading, and a call-to-action button.

How it works

You give it one image (or a video link) and some text. The block stretches the image to fill the width of the screen, overlays your heading/text on top, and adds a pause/play button automatically if you used a video instead of an image.

When authors use it

Use once, at the very top of a page — landing pages, model pages, campaign pages.

What you fill in

Image or videoBackground media. A still image, or a link ending in .mp4 for a looping background video.
HeadingMain title, shown large.
Subheading / body textOptional short supporting line.
CTA linkOptional button, e.g. "Discover Tesi H2".
