Form

Configurable contact/lead forms, built from a linked field-definition sheet.

A configurable form (e.g. contact or lead-generation forms), built from field definitions kept in a separate data sheet rather than typed directly into the page.

How it works

You link the block to a form-definition sheet; that sheet lists each field's type, label, and whether it's required, plus where submissions should be sent. The block builds the actual HTML form, labels, and validation/error messages from that sheet.

When authors use it

Use for contact forms, test-ride requests, newsletter sign-ups — anywhere you need visitor input.

What you fill in

Form sheet linkPath to the linked form-definition document.
