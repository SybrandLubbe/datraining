News / Stories

Card-style news/story feed, purpose-built heading size for news items.

A card-style list of news items or stories, visually similar to Cards but with its own heading size, purpose-built for news feeds.

How it works

Same row-based authoring as Cards: an image cell plus a text cell per news item.

When authors use it

Use on a News/Press page, or a "Latest news" section on the homepage.

What you fill in

Story imageOne image per story.
Story bodyHeading + short summary per story.
