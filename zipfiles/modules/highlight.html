Highlight

Full-width image slider with a scale/zoom reveal effect.

A full-width image slider where each image scales/zooms subtly as it comes into view.

How it works

Add one image per slide. As the visitor scrolls the block into view, each image animates in with a gentle scale effect; the aspect ratio adapts automatically between mobile and desktop.

When authors use it

Use for a striking, editorial-style image showcase (e.g. a photo essay of a motorcycle in detail).
