Text

Plain rich-text section — paragraphs, links, buttons, no special layout.

Plain rich-text content — paragraphs, links, and buttons — with no special layout of its own.

How it works

Whatever you type renders close to how you typed it: headings become styled headings, a bold sentence on its own becomes a styled pull-quote, and any linked "button" text becomes a real button.

When authors use it

Use for straightforward copy that doesn't need an image alongside it (for that, use Text with Image).

What you fill in

Body copyAny mix of headings, paragraphs, links.
