Learn to build Bimota pages with Adobe Edge Delivery & da.live

This site teaches you how to find blocks, assemble pages for any country/language, and publish changes to bimota.com — no code required.

Quick start

1. Understand DA

Read what da.live is and how it relates to bimota.com's Edge Delivery site.

2. Explore the blocks

Open the Blocks Library — it loads the live block/template manifests and lets you try one out.

3. Build a page

Walk through a full /it/it example page, then use the guide's copy/paste snippets for your own locale.

What's in this training site

📘 Author Guide

docs/authors.md — the full manual: what DA is, how to assemble pages, localization, preview/publish steps, troubleshooting, and best practices.

🧩 Blocks Library

pages/blocks.html — live-loaded catalogue of every block and template, with copyable snippets and a "try in page" form.

🌍 Example pages

Full mock pages for /it/it, /us/en, and /de/de showing localization and fallback patterns.
